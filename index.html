Academic Research
Advisor: Prof. Guilin Qi and Senior Lecturer Yuanfang Li (Monash University) 12/2018
Proposed a neural-symbolic complex question answering (NS-CQA) system, which contains (a) a neural
"generator", a sequence-to-sequence model that transforms the natural language query into primitive action
sequences and (b) a symbolic "executor" that performs discrete executions. By employing REINFORCE
method, the task reward of the structured executions can be directly optimized. Recently, we are doing the
experiments.
Knowledge Base Relation Detection via Memory Networks (In progress) Academic Research
Advisor: Prof. Guilin Qi 12/2018
Proposed a relation detection model based on memory networks. Different from previous methods, when we
calculate the similarity between given question Q and a given relation R, we also take the similarity between Q
and the questions whose corresponding relation is R into account.
Chinese Predicate Mention Extraction Academic Research
Advisor: Prof. Guilin Qi 10/2018
